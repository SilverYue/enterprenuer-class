    • Direction to use

    Step 1

    Prepare the Fabric

    Ensure the fabric is clean and dry before applying the spray. Smooth out any major wrinkles with your hands.

    Step 2

    Shake the Bottle Well

    Give the spray bottle a good shake to mix the contents thoroughly.

    Step 3

    Apply the Anti-Wrinkle Spray

    Hold the bottle about 6-8 inches away from the fabric. Evenly spritz the spray over the entire surface of the cloth, focusing on areas prone to wrinkling.

    Step 5

    Allow It to Dry

    Let the fabric air dry completely. This will help set the anti-wrinkle properties of the spray.
